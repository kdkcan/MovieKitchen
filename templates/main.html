How It Works?

Select the Importance Level of Your Team Members

Use sliders in order to select which members of the team you trust more. You should give a ratio between Cast - Director, Director - Scenarist and Cast - Scenarist to determine which side of the slider is more important for you. But, be careful not to make any discrepancy!

Portfolio or Popularity?

Again, use slider between Portfolio Score and Popularity Score in order to help us, which sub-criteria is more important for you in our calculation. Portfolio score is an average score of a member's all works in his career which is calculated by using IMDB ratings. Popularity score is the popularity level of the member which is calculated by using Instagram and Twitter followers' number.

Select Your Team Members

At last, Enter the members of 3 teams to find which one of the teams would be more successful. You should enter an estimated budget (in Millions) for each of your team. This will help you to make the most logical pick. **You should fill all the fields to see the result.

The Result

In order to see the result, you should click "Submit" button after you fill all of the fields. When you clicked the "Submit" button, you will see a chart which shows all of the teams on success rate and budget dimensions. It will help you to compare the teams and their estimated budget. Do not forget that success rate declares a numerical priority between the teams. It does not provide a ratio or linear based comparison. Good luck!
